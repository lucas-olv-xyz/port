Premiere Pro Extension – Reused Content Automation

A custom JavaScript-powered panel built for Adobe Premiere Pro to automate the editing process of reused content. The extension integrates directly into the editing software and executes most of the repetitive editing tasks automatically.

Before the automation, editing each video took around 15 minutes. After the extension was implemented, that time dropped to just 2 minutes — significantly boosting team productivity and improving the company’s output and revenue.

Technologies used:

JavaScript React (UI) CSInterface HostScript.jsx Adobe CEP

Status: Actively used by the video editing team, helping accelerate production and increase delivery volume.
